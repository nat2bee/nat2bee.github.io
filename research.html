Research Interest

Using modern sequencing tools and a LOT of bioinformatics I am trying to understand more about the molecular evolution of the social behaviour. Many bees have been my closely companions in this path but I would love to know more aboute any other social insect. My PhD study is about Expression of Genes Involved in Social Behaviour of Bees with Different Levels of Eusociality, but I am also involved in other side projects such as: Expression of genes involved in worker subcastes; Trancriptomic analyses in the oil-collecting bee Tetrapedia diversipes; Genes involved in the evolution of parasitic behaviour in bees; Analyses of mitochondrial genome in bees.
